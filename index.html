What is this page all about?

This is a small website designed to describe the Network of Hollywood celebreties. I have walked on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and acted like a true tourist, lying down on the street and taking pictures with the stars. But are some stars more prestiges than others? Who are the most famous stars? Who is the STAR of stars? This website describes a project the examines the stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

In the project a Network of Hollywood stars will be created and by looking at this more closely some curious things can be said about the celebreties.

Data from Wikipedia

There are plenty of information on Hollywood celebrities on Wikipedia. Since the basis for the project is list of stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame found on Wikipedia it makes sense to also download their individual wikipages while we are at it.

We are also looking at awards .... The considered awards are:

  • Academy awards (Film): best actor, best actress, best supporting actor, best supporting actress
  • Primetime Emmy Awards (Television): lead actor comedy, lead actress comedy, lead actor drama, lead actress drama
  • Radio Academy's Hall of Fame (Radio): Members and Fellows
  • Tony Awards (Live performance): leading actor play, leading actress play, leading actor musical, leading actress musical
  • Grammy Awards (Recording): Album of the Year, Record of the Year, Song of the Year, Best New Artist
